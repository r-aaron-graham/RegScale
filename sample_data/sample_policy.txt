Title: User Account Management Policy (AC-2)
Framework: NIST 800-53
Control: AC-2

Purpose:
To define requirements for creating, managing, and reviewing user accounts within the organizationâ€™s information systems.

Policy:
- All user accounts must be uniquely assigned to individuals and reviewed quarterly.
- Management approval is required for the creation and deactivation of all user accounts.
- Accounts not used for 90 days must be disabled.
- Temporary accounts shall be automatically disabled after the expiration date.

Procedures:
- Quarterly reviews are performed by the Compliance Team and documented in the audit system.
- Access requests and deactivations are logged and require manager sign-off.
- Review evidence must be retained for a minimum of one year for audit purposes.
